Settings is the phone's system app — and the prototype's one deliberate break
in the fourth wall. Its **Proto Settings** screen hosts the study controls
that used to live outside the phone frame: the sim clock (+1h), the point-of-
view switcher (embody any resident), the device switcher, the assistant brain
toggle (mock / llm dry-run), world reset, session export, and the scenario
player. It declares no actions: nothing here is part of the simulated world's
action space — these are the researcher's levers, not the owner's.
